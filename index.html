About Us

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Lamis Kadhum an accomplished professional with a diverse background in both the arts and engineering. With Iraqi and Swedish roots, I bring a unique cultural perspective to my work. I am an experienced lecturer, job coach, freelance journalist, and architect, with a PhD in Engineering Sciences from Moscow.

As the director of a Cultural Integration Association, I’ve been deeply involved in bridging cultural gaps and providing consultation services to foreign associations in Malmö, Sweden. My passion for writing has led me to publish several novels and books that explore themes of integration, nostalgia, and cross-cultural dynamics. My work has been featured in both Arabic and Swedish, and I have written screenplays that tackle important societal issues, including the role of women, the impact of drugs, and the challenges faced by Arab families in Sweden. In addition to my professional and literary pursuits, I am an active member of multiple prestigious writer and author unions in both Sweden and the Arab world. Through my experience and creativity, I aim to inspire meaningful conversations and foster understanding across cultures. I am always open to new collaborations and projects that promote cultural exchange and social integration.
